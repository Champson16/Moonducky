Tag Maker Overview

Design your own personalized hangtag for your Small Potatoes toy in the Tag Maker.

You can paint, draw, and use stamps or textures to create a completely unique design!

All tag designs can be saved and edited again later.

Note: The Magic Toy Maker is constantly being updated with new costumes and art for different occasions, therefore the Help will differ slightly from what you see in the app.

A blank canvas is the default, to begin your design choose from one of the tools.
With the Canvas tool selected, scroll through the backgrounds palette on the right. Tap one to have it fill the canvas.

Choose the Stamp Tool, then choose a stamp. Use your fingers to position and pinch to resize it.

Choose a Pencil or Paintbrush tool to write or draw. You can also choose a color from the palette on the left.

When your hangtag is finished, tap the screen to have the Small Potato icon appear in the upper left corner.
Choose the Save icon from the Navigation bar.

Create and Save as many hangtags as you like.

When your hangtag is finished, tap the screen to have the Small Potato icon appear in the upper left corner.
From here you can choose the Hat icon to personalize a toy, or the Shopping Cart icon to proceed with your order.

Using the Art Tools

All of the Art Tools are above the canvas area.

Tap the Canvas Tool to access the blank canvas and other images available to load as backgrounds onto the canvas.

Tap an image to move it to the canvas.

The Drawing tool offers crayons, markers, pens, and pencils.

The Paintbrush tool offers a variety of brush styles with the ability to control the size and shape of the stroke.

To change the size of the stroke hold a finger over the brush until the box with slider appears. Select and slide to change shape and size options.

Tap and scroll the color palatte to choose the color for the drawing or painting tool.

The color is used when you paint or draw using your finger.

Tap and scroll to choose texture patterns.

Tap to choose a texture.

Tap back to change the color of the texture.

Using the Stamp and Eraser Tools

The Shape tool allows you to choose a shape and fill it with colors and textures from the palatte.
Tap a shape to have it appear in the center of the canvas.

Pinch or stretch with two fingers to resize and rotate the shape.

The Stamp tool provides embellishments that can be placed anywhere you choose on the canvas.

Pinch or stretch with two fingers to resize and rotate it.

The toys you saved, have also become stamps. Choose the Small Potatoes icon to select one.

Tap the Eraser to remove drawings, stamps, or shapes.
When using the drawing or painting tool tap the eraser and then move your finger over the lines you wish to erase.

Using the Magic Toy Maker Action Bar

To access the Magic Toy Maker Action Bar, tap anywhere and then tap the Small Potatoes icon in the upper left corner.

The Magic Toy Maker Action Bar appears.

Tap Home to return to the Magic Toy Factory train.

Tap the Hat icon to go to the Toy Maker.

Tap the Shopping Cart icon to go to the Order Room.

Tap the Fat Red Couch icon to view other apps from FatRedCouch.

Tap the Question Mark icon to get Help. Of course, you probably already figured that out.

Tap the Save icon to save the current tag.

Tap the Load icon to load the tags you've saved.

Tap the Circle icon to reset the current tag.

Now that you know all the basics – Enjoy and have fun!
