Dressing Room Help

The Dressing Room will let you design your own personalized MoonDucky costumes!

You can change the costuming any way you want by selecting the type of costume you want to change and then scrolling through and choosing one of the selections presented.

Or you can tap the Mystery Box to get a completely random design!

You can save your costume design anytime and re-load a previously saved one for editing. All costume designs can not only be edited again later but you can use the costume for characters in your MoonDucky music videos that you create in the Music Theatre.
