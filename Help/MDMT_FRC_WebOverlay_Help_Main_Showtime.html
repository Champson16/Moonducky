MoonDucky Music Theatre Showtime! Help

Showtime! lets you sit back and enjoy the performances you've created using the MoonDucky Music Theatre app.

After you've arranged a show in Rehearsal and created a Performance for Showtime!, you can watch it again and again anytime. While a show is being presented, you can restart it at the beginning or exit to the Lobby at any time.

Just select the Performance you want to watch and enjoy! At the end, after the curtain falls, and you will be returned to the Lobby.
